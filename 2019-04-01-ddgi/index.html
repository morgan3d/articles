**Dynamic Diffuse Global Illumination** In April 2019, this page will contain details on implementing Dynamic Diffuse Global Illumination (DDGI) using ray-traced irradiance probes with visibility. The technique was first presented at GTC'19 and GDC'19 in the NVIDIA sponsored sessions. It will be published in a peer-reviewed research paper by Majercik et al. in JCGT with our academic collaborators at McGill and the University of Montreal and presented for an academic audience at I3D'19 in May. Game developers can contact Morgan McGuire @CasualEffects for more information in the mean time.
