**Banjo Chord Chart** Morgan McGuire Published 2024-01-12, Updated 2024-01-13 ![](banjo.webp) All chords in gDGBD (standard Open G) tuning. In Open G tuning, a banjo only has three distinct types of strings: B (2), G (3), and then three D strings (1, 4, 5). This means that most chords will duplicate the fret position on strings 1, 4, and 5, or more commonly, leave the 5th string open or muted as a G note. # Fretboard
